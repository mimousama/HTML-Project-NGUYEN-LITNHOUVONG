An excellent program

EFREI is a premier French "Grande École" specialized in digital engineering. Since its integration as a component of Paris-Panthéon-Assas University, the school has established a unique academic ecosystem where high-level technical expertise meets the legal, economic, and social excellence of France's flagship university. The IT Department stands at the core of this mission, producing agile engineers capable of navigating an increasingly complex technological landscape.

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure

EFREI provides a cutting-edge learning environment designed to foster innovation and hands-on experience. Our campus features high-tech laboratories, collaborative workspaces, and specialized IT facilities that mirror the standards of the modern tech industry. From advanced robotics labs to secure networking hubs, students have access to the latest hardware and software tools, ensuring they transition seamlessly from academic theory to professional practice. This modern infrastructure is the cornerstone of our "learning by doing" philosophy, offering an inspiring setting where tomorrow's digital leaders can build, test, and scale their ideas.
