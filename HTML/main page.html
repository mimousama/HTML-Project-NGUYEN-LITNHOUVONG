An excellent program

EFREI is a premier French "Grande École" specialized in digital engineering. Since its integration as a component of Paris-Panthéon-Assas University, the school has established a unique academic ecosystem where high-level technical expertise meets the legal, economic, and social excellence of France's flagship university. The IT Department stands at the core of this mission, producing agile engineers capable of navigating an increasingly complex technological landscape.
